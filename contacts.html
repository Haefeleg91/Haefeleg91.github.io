About me

My name is Greg Haefele, I am a 26 year old Engineering student at UNH Manchester. I have an associates in Mechanical Design Technology from Nashua Community College and I'm currently working on my Bachelors degree and I have been working with various CADD programs for the past 10 years or so. This robot has been a passion project for me during that time, starting all the way back in high-school and it started as just a proof of concept. During my time at NCC and UNH I have gathered skills the create a working model that's actually physically possible, when it was first created most of the parts couldn't actually fit together. The concept came to me from a show that I watched as a kid in high-school and I thought it would be cool to make in CADD, I wasn't worried about if the robot could actually fit together or not. I am still working on this page and this project in general so if you have any questions please feel free to contact me below.
